About SignalCraft Audio

Born from the desire to blend artistry and engineering, SignalCraft Audio creates boutique guitar pedals that respond like instruments themselves — expressive, reliable, and full of character.

Each pedal is hand-tested in East London using top-grade components, ensuring exceptional tone and build quality for musicians who demand the best.
