About SignalCraft Audio

We craft boutique pedals that blend analog warmth with precision engineering — designed and tested by musicians, for musicians.

Our Story

SignalCraft Audio was founded by musicians and engineers who share a deep love for tone. We wanted pedals that respond to touch and dynamics like instruments — not just effects. From our East London workshop, every pedal is hand-tested and built to inspire creativity.

Our Philosophy

Hand-Tested Quality

Each unit is built, wired, and tested by hand to ensure sonic perfection and reliability.

Small-Batch Craft

We believe in limited production — not mass manufacturing — to ensure each pedal is unique and personal.

Musician Collaboration

From concept to testing, we collaborate with artists to craft sounds that elevate their music.
