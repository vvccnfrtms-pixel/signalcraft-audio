About SignalCraft Audio

At SignalCraft Audio, we handcraft boutique guitar pedals with precision and passion. Every pedal is designed and tested in East London, South Africa, for musicians who demand premium tone.

Our Story

Founded by passionate musicians and engineers, SignalCraft Audio was born out of the desire to create pedals that deliver exceptional tone, feel, and reliability. We combine analog expertise with modern design to craft pedals that inspire performance in both studio and live settings.

Our Philosophy

Hand-Tested Quality

Every pedal is individually tested to ensure it meets our rigorous standards for tone and performance.

Small-Batch Production

We focus on limited runs to maintain quality, uniqueness, and exclusivity for our customers.

Artist Collaboration

We work closely with musicians to craft pedals that suit the needs of professionals and enthusiasts alike.
